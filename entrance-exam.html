Entrance Examination

Entrance examinations comes up every september against the resemption of a new section.

Papers are taken in Mathematics (including Quantitative Aptitude),
English (including Verbal Reasoning), and
General paper (Current affairs, Social studies and Science).
General paper is not taken by international students.


FCASS strictly condemns exam malpractice and automatically disqualifies any candidate found gaulty.




Requirements on the Entrance Examination day.


Candidates must bring the following to the examination hall:
1. Examination slip
2. Examination materials-Pens, Mathematical set, Ruler, Eraser, Sharpener and HB pencil.

List of successful candidates will be published 48hours after the exam.
